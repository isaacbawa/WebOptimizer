Why is Performance Important?

Website performance plays a critical role in user engagement, conversion rates, and search engine rankings. A slow or unresponsive website can frustrate visitors, leading to higher bounce rates and missed opportunities. Our Performance Audit ensures your website delivers optimal speed and responsiveness to keep users satisfied and engaged.

Did you know that over half of all users abandon a site if it takes more than three seconds to load? Research has shown that even a single second of delay can lead to a 7% drop in conversions, costing businesses millions annually.
